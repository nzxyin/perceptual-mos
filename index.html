Naturalness MOS Test

Participant Instructions

Thank you for participating in this listening study.

In this task, you will hear a series of short speech recordings. After listening to each recording, you will rate how natural the speech sounds.

What “Naturalness” Means

Naturalness refers to how much the speech sounds like it was produced by a real human speaking in a normal, everyday situation.

When rating naturalness, consider:

  • Does the voice sound human-like?
  • Does the rhythm and intonation sound natural?
  • Does the speech flow smoothly?
  • Are there any robotic, metallic, distorted, or artificial qualities?

Rating Scale

You will rate each sample using the following 5-point scale:

1 – Very unnatural (highly robotic or artificial)

2 – Unnatural

3 – Neutral / somewhat natural

4 – Natural

5 – Very natural (indistinguishable from a real human speaker)

Choose the rating that best reflects your overall impression of the sample.

Listening Guidelines

  • Use headphones in a quiet environment if possible.
  • Listen to each sample carefully before rating.
  • You may replay the sample if replay is allowed.
  • Trust your first overall impression — there are no right or wrong answers.
  • Rate each sample independently.

Your responses are anonymous and will be used only for research purposes.

Thank you for your participation.
